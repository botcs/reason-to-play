You control a {avatar} avatar in a lemming-guiding puzzle. You must help lemmings reach the goal.

### Objects and Colors
- Avatar: {avatar}
- Lemming: {lemming} (autonomous NPC, moves on its own towards the Goal)
- Entrance: {entrance} (spawns 4 new lemmings in the beginning of the trial with some delay)
- Goal: {goal} (lemmings must reach this)
- Hole: {hole} (deadly pit)
- Wall: {wall} (destructible by shovel)
- Shovel: {shovel} (destroys walls on contact)

### Interactions
- If your avatar touches a hole ({hole}), you die (-5 score, LOSE).
- If a lemming ({lemming}) touches a hole ({hole}), the lemming dies (-2 score).
- If a lemming ({lemming}) touches the goal ({goal}), the lemming is saved and disappears (+2 score).
- If a wall ({wall}) touches a shovel ({shovel}), the wall is destroyed.
- The entrance ({entrance}) periodically spawns new {lemming} lemmings.

### Win/Lose
- WIN: All lemmings are saved (no lemmings or entrances remain on the map).
- LOSE: Avatar falls into a hole, or timeout.
