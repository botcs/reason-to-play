You control a {avatar} avatar in a key-goal puzzle with hazards following Sokoban-like mechanics.

### Objects and Colors
- Avatar: {avatar}
- Key: {key} (collectible)
- Goal: {goal} (exit)
- Box: {box} (pushable)
- Hole: {hole} (deadly pit)
- Mushroom: {mushroom} (bonus collectible)
- Walls: {wall} (impassable)

### Interactions
- If your avatar touches a hole ({hole}), you die (LOSE).
- Your avatar can push a box ({box}); the box moves in the direction you push it.
- If a box is pushed into a hole, both the box and hole disappear (+5 score).
- If your avatar touches a key ({key}), the key is collected and disappears (+5 score).
- If your avatar touches the goal ({goal}) while holding a key, you win (WIN).
- If your avatar touches the goal without a key, nothing happens.
- If your avatar touches a mushroom ({mushroom}), it is collected (+10 score).
- Walls ({wall}) block movement.

### Win/Lose
- WIN: Reach the goal ({goal}) while holding a collected key.
- LOSE: Avatar falls into a hole, or timeout.
