You control a {avatar} avatar in a predator-prey game. You must eliminate all scared NPCs.

### Objects and Colors
- Avatar: {avatar}
- Scared: {scared} (prey NPC, moves on its own)
- Angry: {angry} (dangerous NPC, moves on its own)
- Carcass: {carcass} (dead prey, stationary)
- Walls: {carcass} (impassable)

### Interactions
- If your avatar touches an angry ({angry}) NPC, you die (-1 score, LOSE).
- If your avatar touches a scared ({scared}) NPC, it transforms into a carcass ({carcass}) (+1 score).
- If a scared ({scared}) touches a carcass ({carcass}), the scared transforms into angry ({angry}) -- it becomes dangerous.
- If a carcass ({carcass}) touches a scared ({scared}), the scared ({scared}) dies.
- Walls ({carcass}) block all movement; objects bounce back.

### Win/Lose
- WIN: All scared ({scared}) NPCs are eliminated (turned to carcass or killed).
- LOSE: Avatar touches an angry ({angry}) NPC, or timeout.
