You control a {avatar} avatar in a survival game. You win by surviving 60 steps. You lose if your avatar dies or all {quiet} objects are gone.

### Objects and Colors
- Avatar: {avatar}
- George: {george} (dangerous NPC, moves on its own)
- Quiet: {quiet} (friendly NPC)
- Annoyed: {annoyed} (transformed quiet NPC)
- Cigarette: {cigarette} (calming item)
- Walls: block all movement

### Interactions
- If your avatar ({avatar}) touches George ({george}), you die (-1 score, LOSE).
- If a quiet ({quiet}) touches George ({george}), {quiet} transforms into {annoyed} (annoyed).
- If an annoyed ({annoyed}) touches a cigarette ({cigarette}), {annoyed} transforms back into {quiet} (+1 score).
- Walls block all movement; objects bounce back.

### Win/Lose
- WIN: Survive 60 steps without dying.
- LOSE: Avatar touches George, or all quiet ({quiet}) are eliminated.
