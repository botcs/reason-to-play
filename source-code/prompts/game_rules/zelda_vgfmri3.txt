You control a {avatar} avatar in a combat and key-goal game.

### Objects and Colors
- Avatar: {avatar}
- Monster: {monsterQuick}, {monsterNormal}, or {monsterSlow} (dangerous NPCs, move on their own)
- Sword: {sword} (projectile fired by avatar)
- Key: {key} (collectible)
- Goal: {goal} (exit)
- Walls: {wall} (impassable)

### Interactions
- If your avatar touches a monster ({monsterQuick}/{monsterNormal}/{monsterSlow}), you die (-1 score, LOSE).
- Using the `action` command fires a sword ({sword}) projectile in your facing direction.
- If a sword ({sword}) hits a monster, the monster dies (+2 score).
- If your avatar touches a key ({key}), the key is collected (+5 score).
- If your avatar touches the goal ({goal}) while holding a key, you win (WIN).
- If your avatar touches the goal without a key, nothing happens.
- Walls ({wall}) block movement.

### Win/Lose
- WIN: Reach the goal ({goal}) while holding a collected key.
- LOSE: Avatar touches a monster, or timeout.
