You control a {avatar} avatar in a multi-agent coordination puzzle.

### Objects and Colors
- Avatar: {avatar}
- Box1: {box1} (pushable, target for elimination)
- Box2: {box2} (optional collectible by avatar)
- Box3: {box3} (pushable, necessary collectible by chasers)
- Chaser: {chaser1} or {chaser2} (autonomous NPCs that move toward targets)
- Forcefield: {forcefield} (blocks chasers but not avatar)
- Walls: {wall} (impassable)

### Interactions
- Your avatar can push box1 ({box1}) and box3 ({box3}); they move in the push direction.
- If your avatar touches box2 ({box2}), it disappears (+1 score).
- If a chaser ({chaser1}/{chaser2}) touches box1 ({box1}), the box disappears.
- If a chaser touches box3 ({box3}), the box disappears (+1 score).
- Chasers are blocked by forcefields ({forcefield}).
- Walls ({wall}) block movement.

### Win/Lose
- WIN: All box1 ({box1}) objects are eliminated.
- LOSE: Avatar dies or timeout.
